Role – Scrum Master.

Job Summary:
We are seeking an experienced and motivated Scrum Master to join our team. As a Scrum Master, you will be the champion of the Scrum framework, coaching and guiding our development teams to deliver high-quality products during sprints. You will facilitate Scrum ceremonies, remove impediments, and foster a collaborative, self-organizing team environment to ensure project success. The ideal candidate is passionate about Agile methodologies, has excellent communication skills, and holds a recognized Scrum Master certification.

Key Responsibilities:

Facilitate Scrum Ceremonies: Lead and coordinate daily stand-ups, sprint planning, sprint reviews, retrospectives, and backlog refinement sessions to ensure effective team collaboration and alignment with project goals.
Coach and Mentor: Guide team members and stakeholders on Agile/Scrum principles, practices, and values to promote continuous improvement and adherence to the Scrum framework.
Remove Impediments: Identify and resolve obstacles that hinder the team’s progress, ensuring the team remains focused on sprint goals (e.g., addressing technical issues like server instability).
Foster Collaboration: Work closely with Product Owners to manage product backlogs, prioritize tasks, and align team efforts with business objectives. Promote a safe and transparent environment for problem-solving and decision-making.
Improve Team Performance: Use empirical data (e.g., sprint velocity, burndown charts) to assess team maturity, optimize performance, and drive higher productivity.
Ensure Agile Adherence: Educate and support the organization in adopting Scrum and other Agile practices (e.g., Kanban, XP) while maintaining flexibility to adapt to team needs.
Stakeholder Communication: Act as a liaison between the Scrum team, Product Owner, and external stakeholders to ensure transparency and alignment on project progress.
Promote Continuous Improvement: Facilitate retrospectives to identify areas for improvement and implement actionable changes to enhance team workflows and processes.
Tool Utilization: Leverage Agile project management tools (e.g., Jira, Trello, Rally) to track progress, manage backlogs, and maintain transparency.
Qualifications and Skills:

Education: Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, Business, or a related field is preferred but may be waived with significant relevant experience (e.g., 5+ years in a similar role).
Experience:
Minimum of 2–3 years of experience as a Scrum Master for a software development team diligently applying Scrum principles and practices.
Experience with scaled Agile frameworks (e.g., SAFe, LeSS) is a plus.
Prior experience in software development or technology-related roles is beneficial for understanding team dynamics.
Certifications:
Required: Certified ScrumMaster® (CSM) from Scrum Alliance or Professional Scrum Master™ (PSM I or PSM II) from Scrum.org.
Preferred: Advanced certifications such as Advanced Certified ScrumMaster (A-CSM), Certified Scrum Professional®-ScrumMaster (CSP-SM), SAFe Scrum Master, or PMI-Agile Certified Practitioner (PMI-ACP).
Skills:
Deep knowledge of Agile methodologies (Scrum, Kanban, XP, etc.) and their artifacts (e.g., user stories, burndown charts, definition of done).
Exceptional communication, facilitation, and conflict-resolution skills.
Strong leadership and servant leadership qualities to empower self-organizing teams.
Proficiency with Agile tools such as Jira, Trello, or Rally.
Analytical and problem-solving skills to anticipate and address risks or impediments.
Ability to coach teams remotely or in-person and adapt to diverse stakeholder needs.
